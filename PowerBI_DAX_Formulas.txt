Power BI Dashboard Guide

1. Open Power BI Desktop.
2. Get Data -> CSV -> select data/business_sales_data.csv (or import your downloaded Microsoft sample .pbix/.xlsx).
3. Build relationships between tables if you imported multiple tables.
4. Recommended visuals:
   - Line chart: Revenue over time (OrderDate)
   - Stacked bar: Revenue by Region and ProductCategory
   - Matrix: Top products by revenue and profit
   - Map: Region revenue (if you have geo fields)
5. Add slicers: Region, ProductCategory, CustomerSegment

DAX examples (add to Power BI as new measures):
Total Revenue = SUM('business_sales_data'[Revenue])
Total Profit = SUM('business_sales_data'[Profit])
Profit Margin = DIVIDE([Total Profit], [Total Revenue])
Previous Month Revenue = CALCULATE([Total Revenue], PREVIOUSMONTH('business_sales_data'[OrderDate]))
MoM Growth % = DIVIDE([Total Revenue] - [Previous Month Revenue], [Previous Month Revenue])
